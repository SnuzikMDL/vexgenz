1. General Provisions

1.1 These rules are compiled by a representative of the Vexgenz project and apply to all game modes of the Vexgenz project. Ignorance of the rules does not exempt violators from responsibility and may aggravate the punishment.

1.2 The Vexgenz project is a server located at the following address: vexgenz.minefort.com. All other domains are not affiliated. By joining our project, you automatically accept these rules, as well as the rules of other game modes.

1.3 The rules may be changed without explicit notification to the main project audience. Significant rule changes will be announced on the project’s Discord server.

1.4 Decisions and actions of the main administration (scamingdev (SnuzikMDL), wspr1) are not subject to discussion or appeal. The main administration has the right to issue punishments outside the scope of these rules.

1.5 The server administration does not issue in-game resources or compensation for losses due to server kicks, lags, bugs, or actions of players using prohibited software (cheats).

2. Joining Rules

3. Purchase of Paid Services

3.1 Playing on the project does not require payment for the use of Vexgenz resources.

3.2 Players may voluntarily pay a certain amount to the project for in-game services unilaterally.

3.3 In case of payment errors or non-delivery of in-game services after payment, contact support with the electronic receipt sent to your email. Without a receipt, recovery services will be denied. The project does not resend receipts for security reasons. Keep your receipt at the time of payment or find it in your email.

3.4 There are no refunds for purchases, as Vexgenz is not a commercial organization, and all purchases are considered voluntary donations for server development.

3.5 Purchasing premium groups does not grant immunity from these rules or their violations.

4. In-Game Communication

4.1 Flooding (sending more than five similar or identical messages in a row within one minute) is prohibited. [5 hours mute]

4.2 Coordinating with other players or using alternate accounts to send the same message in a short time to bypass flooding penalties is prohibited. [5 hours mute for each participant]

4.3 Flooding with symbols (e.g., “!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!” or excessive laughter) is prohibited. [5 hours mute]

4.4 Organizing flooding through polls (e.g., “send + in chat”) is prohibited, except for requests to join/sell/trade resources or math problems (no more than 5 problems in 5 minutes). Exceptions include messages like “who wants to be team + in chat.” [5 hours mute]

4.5 If 5 or more people respond to a flood poll, the organizer gets a [10 hours mute], and responders get a [5 hours mute].
